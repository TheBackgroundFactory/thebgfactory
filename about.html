About Us

The Background Factory is a website that offers several dynamic javascript backgrounds for web development integration. It was conceived during Local Hack Day by a group of students at McMaster University who were interested in creative visual simulations. Currently, the "Dot Matrix", "Dynamic Pixels", and "Dot Wave" are available for display and download.
If you would like to leave a comment or contribute your own ideas, please visit our GitHub page
Enjoy our site!
